## Heyo!

I am currently an undergraduate student in my penultimate year at the University of Sydney. As an aspiring polymath, my majors are in: Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, and Econometrics with minors in Business Analytics, Economics, and Finance.

I have co-founded and am currently the co-president of the [ANOVA Project](http://anovaprojectusyd.org). We are the first student pro-bono STEM consulting society in the world. Whether you're an organisation that wants to help give the next generation of STEM students an opportunity at building their careers or a keen bean STEM student interested in developing your skills, feel free to reach out to me!

### About myself
I'm currently exploring the blogosphere and contributing with my knowledge on the intersection of Machine Learning, Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, and Econometrics. I am currently a developer a research assistant in Computational Economics at [QuantEcon](https://quantecon.org/), machine learning engineer, head tutor at the Econometrics department, and sleep deprived student.

This blog comprises of my (hopefully decent) attempts at explaining such concepts, messing around with some data, and noting down my own musings.

I'm quite the voracious reader and you can catch me reading anything from the latest _Murakami_ novel to _Elements of Statistical Learning_. Other hobbies include musicals, anime,  teaching, and drinking copious amounts of coffee.
