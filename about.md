## Hey there!

I am currently an undergraduate student in my penultimate year at the University of Sydney. As an aspiring polymath, my majors are: Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, and Econometrics with minors in Business Analytics, Economics, and Finance.

I have co-founded and am currently the co-president of the [ANOVA Project](http://anovaprojectusyd.org.s3-website-ap-southeast-2.amazonaws.com/). We are the first student pro-bono STEM consulting society in the world.

### About myself
I'm currently exploring the blogosphere and contributing with my knowledge on the intersection of both Machine Learning and Computer Science with applications to various domains.

This blog comprises of my (hopefully decent) attempts at explaining such concepts, messing around with some data, and noting down my own musings.


I'm quite the voracious reader and you can catch me reading anything from the latest _Murakami_ novel to _Elements of Statistical Learning_. Other hobbies include musicals, teaching, drinking copious amounts of coffee, and anime.
